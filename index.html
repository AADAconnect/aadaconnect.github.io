What's inside

Built for real smart homes

Every feature is designed to give you precise, reliable control with zero cloud dependency and a minimal, purposeful interface.

Real-time MQTT Control
Commands reach your ESP32 devices in milliseconds over a persistent WSS connection — no polling, no delay.
Dynamic Room Filtering
Organize devices by room with a slide-up Rooms panel. Drill into any space and manage its plugs independently.
Dark & Light Themes
Cross-tab synchronized theme preference stored in localStorage. Instant toggle, no flash, consistent across all pages.
Secure Authentication
Broker credentials are never stored in plaintext. Session state is kept in memory with a clean login/logout flow.
Mobile-First Design
Designed and refined for phone-native interaction — bottom sheets, tap-to-confirm, haptic-ready, no pinch zoom.
Live Device Status
Online/offline state, last-seen timestamps, and on/off status are reflected live without any manual refresh.
How it works

Simple setup, powerful control

01
Create an account
Sign up on AADAconnect and log in. Your credentials are used to connect securely to your MQTT broker — no cloud, no middleman.
02
Power on your device
Plug in your AADA device. On first boot it broadcasts its own Wi-Fi hotspot — no flashing or cables needed.
03
Tap + and connect to the device hotspot
In the dashboard, tap the + button to add a new device. The Wi-Fi Setup page will open — connect your phone to the plug's hotspot when prompted.
04
Share your Wi-Fi credentials
On the Wi-Fi Setup page, enter your home network name and password. The device saves them, connects to your network, and appears live on your dashboard instantly.

Architecture

No cloud. No server. Just your devices.

AADAconnect is a fully static web app. It runs entirely in the browser, connects directly to your MQTT broker over a secure WebSocket, and talks straight to your ESP32 devices — nothing in between.
